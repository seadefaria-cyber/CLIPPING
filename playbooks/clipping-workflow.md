# Clipping Workflow

The daily flow from raw footage to published posts.

## Daily Flow

| Step | Who | What | Tool |
|------|-----|------|------|
| 1 | Sean | Prepares and sends footage to editors | Dropbox |
| 2 | Editors | Edit clips (2/day each) | CapCut |
| 3 | Editors | Upload finished clips to Dropbox | Dropbox |
| 4 | Editors | Post to their assigned accounts | Native apps |
| 5 | Charlie | Cross-posts his clips to FB + YouTube Shorts | Native apps |
| 6 | Editors | Screenshot daily analytics in group chat | Group chat |

## Weekly Flow

| Step | Who | What | Tool |
|------|-----|------|------|
| 1 | Metricool | Auto-generates weekly performance report | Metricool |
| 2 | Sean | Forwards PDF report to client | Email (2 min) |
| 3 | Lukas | Reviews top performers, flags trends | Dashboard |
