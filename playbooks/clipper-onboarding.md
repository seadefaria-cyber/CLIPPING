# Clipper Onboarding

How to onboard a new Fiverr editor into a WLDFR campaign.

## Before They Start

1. Confirm rate ($750/mo for standard Fiverr editors)
2. Assign their platforms (typically 1 TikTok + 1 Instagram account)
3. Share Dropbox folder access (footage library)
4. Add them to the group chat

## Day 1: Setup

1. Send them the "How to Clip" guide (see: how-to-clip.md)
2. Share login credentials for their assigned accounts (via 1Password)
3. Walk through the posting schedule
4. Have them submit a test clip before going live

## Ongoing Requirements

- Post 2x/day on assigned accounts
- Report daily analytics (screenshots in group chat)
- All clips must be mobile-friendly vertical video
- Use CapCut for editing
- Content CANNOT be identical across pages (each page needs unique captions/hooks at minimum)

## If They Miss Posts

- First time: Warning in group chat
- Second time: Direct message, discuss
- Third time: Replace. Fiverr has endless editors.
