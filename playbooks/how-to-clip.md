# How to Clip

Standards and guidelines for creating clips in a WLDFR campaign.

## Format

- Vertical video (9:16)
- 30-90 seconds ideal (under 60 preferred for TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
- Mobile-friendly text overlays
- Edit in CapCut

## What Makes a Good Clip

- Strong hook in the first 2 seconds (visual or text)
- One moment, one emotion. Don't cram multiple highlights.
- Music moments > talking moments (for music artists)
- Crowd reactions add energy
- End on a high note or cliffhanger

## Content Rules

- Content CANNOT be identical across pages
- Each page needs unique captions/hooks at minimum
- No watermarks from other platforms
- No copyrighted music overlays (use the artist's own music)

## Posting Schedule

- Each editor posts 2x/day to their assigned platforms
- Ideal posting times: Morning (8-10 AM) / Evening (5-7 PM)
