# BFPA Editor Brief

## Account
@bodyfocusedprocessaddiction
Platforms: Instagram + TikTok (same content, double upload)
Topic: Body-Focused Process Addiction (skin picking, hair pulling, nail biting, cheek biting)

## Weekly schedule - 3 posts

### Monday - Carousel (2-4 slides)
Question or validation post. First slide has a bold hook with a faded photo watermark behind deep purple. Remaining slides are text on solid deep purple. Last slide is a CTA ("Comment below.").

### Tuesday - Carousel (2-3 slides)
Neuroscience or reframe post. Same format. First slide gets an AI-generated watermark image (nervous system, brain, etc.). Rest are solid purple with text.

### Friday - Video Reel (45-75 sec)
Mom films herself talking to camera. We provide a concept and hook, not a word-for-word script. She speaks naturally. Editor handles the cut.

## Design specs
- Background: deep purple (#2D0050)
- Main text: white (#FFFFFF)
- Accent text: light purple (#BEA6DC)
- Font: Helvetica Neue Bold, centered
- No emojis, no em dashes, no @ handles on slides
- Slide dimensions: 1080x1350 (4:5 ratio)

## Captions
One sentence + hashtags. Keep it simple.

## Tone
Direct, genuine, not corporate, not AI-sounding. Speaks to people living with these conditions, not about them.
