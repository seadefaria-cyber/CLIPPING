# WLDFR Brain

This is the central context file. It gives AI (Claude) and team members a complete picture of how the business operates.

## What is WLDFR?

WLDFR is a social media clipping and digital amplification agency. We take artists' existing content (live performances, interviews, behind-the-scenes footage) and turn it into viral short-form clips distributed across fan pages on TikTok, Instagram, X, Facebook, and YouTube Shorts.

## Business Model

- Charge artists/management $20K+/mo for managed clipping campaigns
- Hire editors (lead editor + Fiverr editors) to produce 6-8+ clips/day
- Distribute across 9-11+ fan page accounts per campaign
- Sean manages, delegates, and reports to client. Does NOT post daily.

## Key Principles

1. Sean should never be posting daily. The system runs itself.
2. Every editor has clear assigned accounts. No overlap.
3. Content cannot be identical across pages.
4. Best-performing clips get cross-posted to Facebook + YouTube Shorts.
5. Weekly automated reports go to the client. Keep it clean and professional.

## Revenue Targets

- First client (Mellencamp): $20K/mo
- Goal: Stack 2-3 clients at $20K+/mo each
- Keep margins above 70%
